# MSME Registration Guide - Complete Step-by-Step Process

## What is Udyam Registration?

Udyam Registration is the government registration process for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in India. This registration provides various benefits and is mandatory for availing government schemes.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Micro Enterprise
- Manufacturing: Investment up to ₹1 crore and turnover up to ₹5 crores
- Service: Investment up to ₹1 crore and turnover up to ₹5 crores

### Small Enterprise  
- Manufacturing: Investment up to ₹10 crores and turnover up to ₹50 crores
- Service: Investment up to ₹10 crores and turnover up to ₹50 crores

### Medium Enterprise
- Manufacturing: Investment up to ₹50 crores and turnover up to ₹250 crores
- Service: Investment up to ₹50 crores and turnover up to ₹250 crores

## Required Documents

1. **Aadhaar Card** - Primary requirement
2. **PAN Card** - For business entity
3. **Business Registration Certificate** (if applicable)
4. **Bank Statement** - For turnover verification
5. **GST Registration** (if applicable)
6. **Import Export Code** (for export businesses)

## Step-by-Step Registration Process

### Step 1: Access the Portal
- Visit: https://udyamregistration.gov.in/
- Click on "Register New MSME"

### Step 2: Enter Basic Details
- Enter Aadhaar number
- Verify OTP sent to registered mobile
- Enter business details

### Step 3: Business Information
- Select business activity
- Enter investment details
- Provide turnover information
- Add employment details

### Step 4: Upload Documents
- Upload required documents
- Ensure all documents are clear and valid

### Step 5: Verification & Submission
- Review all information
- Submit the application
- Download acknowledgment receipt

## Benefits of Registration

1. **Financial Benefits**
   - Access to collateral-free loans
   - Lower interest rates
   - Credit guarantee schemes

2. **Government Schemes**
   - Technology upgradation schemes
   - Export promotion schemes
   - Skill development programs

3. **Tax Benefits**
   - Exemption from certain taxes
   - Reduced documentation requirements

4. **Market Access**
   - Government procurement opportunities
   - Tender participation benefits

## Important Tips

- Keep all documents ready before starting
- Ensure Aadhaar is linked to mobile number
- Double-check all entered information
- Save the registration certificate for future use
